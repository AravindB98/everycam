everycam

Every camera is a robot teacher — privacy-first, robot-ready embodied data from everyday cameras.

View on GitHub ▶ Try the Demo

🧒 What is this? (Plain English)

Robots learn by watching — but robot training videos are rare and expensive. Everycam's idea: the world is already full of cameras (phones, webcams, CCTV). It turns ordinary camera footage into privacy-safe, robot-ready training data, so every camera becomes a robot teacher.

✨ Key Features

Privacy-first

Faces and backgrounds are redacted before anything leaves the device.

Pose & action extraction

Ordinary video becomes structured motion data.

Robot-ready formats

Exports in formats robot-learning pipelines consume directly.

Scale from what exists

Phones, webcams, CCTV — the sensors are already everywhere.
